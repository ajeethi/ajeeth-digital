FAQ

Everything You
Need to Know

Questions we get from business owners before partnering with us. Honest, clear answers — no agency spin.

How quickly can I see results from Meta Ads?
Most clients start seeing measurable results within the first 2–4 weeks. Optimal performance typically builds over 60–90 days as we gather data, test creatives, and refine targeting. We're always transparent about timelines from day one — no false promises.
What is your minimum monthly ad budget?
We recommend a minimum of ₹30,000/month for Meta Ads and ₹20,000/month for Google Ads to generate meaningful results. This is your ad spend — separate from our management fee. During our free strategy call, we'll recommend the right budget for your specific goals and market.
Do you work with small and local businesses?
Absolutely — local businesses are our specialty. We've run successful campaigns for clinics, restaurants, salons, hotels, furniture stores, and more. We create hyper-targeted local campaigns that reach your ideal customers in your specific area — people actively looking for businesses like yours.
What's included in AI Video Ads?
Our AI Video Ads include: script writing, AI-generated visuals, professional voiceovers (Tamil & English), motion graphics, premium editing, captions, and platform-optimized formats for Instagram Reels, Facebook Reels, YouTube Shorts, and WhatsApp Status. Multiple revision rounds included.
How do you measure and report on ROI?
We set up complete tracking (Meta Pixel, Conversion API, GA4, GTM) from day one so you can see exactly where leads and sales come from. You get access to a real-time dashboard showing leads, cost per lead, ROAS, and revenue — plus detailed weekly reports and monthly strategy review calls.
What industries do you specialize in?
We work with clinics, hospitals, physiotherapy centers, hotels, restaurants, furniture businesses, interior designers, educational institutions, construction companies, real estate developers, salons, local businesses, and ecommerce brands. If you serve customers and want more of them, we can help you.
Do you offer a money-back guarantee?
No honest agency can guarantee specific ad performance — results depend on your offer, market, and competition. But we guarantee our process, effort, and complete transparency. We've never had a client leave unsatisfied with how we work, and our results across 100+ businesses speak for themselves.
What happens on the free strategy call?
It's a genuine 30-minute strategy session — not a sales pitch. We discuss your business goals, current marketing challenges, target audience, and budget. By the end, you'll have a clear picture of what's possible and a recommended action plan specific to your business. No obligation, no pressure.
